STEM is an acronym that stands for Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. It refers to a field of study and a type of education that emphasizes the importance of these four disciplines. In educational settings, STEM often refers to a cross-disciplinary approach to teaching, aiming to increase student interest in these fields and improve problem-solving skills.
